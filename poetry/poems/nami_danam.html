Nami Danam

Agar jo tumhe mujhse nafrat na hoti
Toh shayad tum bhi samjhte zara sa

Agar mai tumhare kisi kaam aata
Toh shayad ettiquette rakhte zara sa

Agar raste mere jo tumse milte kabhi
Kuchh dur saath tum bhi chalte zara sa

Waqt ke khajane se do pal churate
Sun.ne ki koshish karte zara sa

Shabiistan ke baher bhi hai ek duniya
Nikal ke tum bhi dekhte zara sa

Intezaar me ek phool sukh gya kabka
Kabhi dekhne tum bhi aate zara sa

Sach bolkar bhi haar gya ek ladka
Dusaro ke jhuth bhi pakdate zara sa

Dost toh yaha khair kon kiska hai
Dushman hi sahi maan lete zara sa

- Raja
