Nanhi Chidiya

Keh dena
Jo kuchh bhi kehna hai
Jaise bhi kehna hai
Jisse bhi kehna hai

Keh dena
Vapas lautne se pehle
Kadam thamne se pehle
Swapn jalne se pehle

Keh dena
Kuch baatein ankahi reh gayi ho agar
Kuch baatein ansuni reh gayi ho agar

Keh dena
Thodi si himmat karke
Thodi si umeed rakhke

Kya pata ek roz baad
Aasman ke anjuman mein dene ko waqt hi na rahe
Kya pata ek roz baad
Zameen ke aanchal mein dene ko zarra hi na rahe
Kya pata ek roz baad
Kuchh bhi kehne sunne ka koi arth hi na rahe

Keh dena - Laut jaane se pehle
Keh dena - Door jaane se pehle

Keh dena
Jo kuch bhi kehna hai
Jaise bhi kehna hai
Jisse bhi kehna hai

- Raja
